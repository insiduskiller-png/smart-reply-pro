# Payment Information

All subscription payments go to the Stripe account associated with your configured:

- `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY`

## Where to see payments

In Stripe Dashboard:

1. Open **Stripe Dashboard**
2. Go to **Payments**

All subscription payment events will appear there.

## Where money is deposited

Stripe automatically deposits funds to the bank account connected in your Stripe payout settings.

To configure bank payouts:

1. Open **Stripe Dashboard**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Go to **Payouts**
4. Add/confirm your **Bank account**
