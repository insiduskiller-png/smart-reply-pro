What this extension uses & why

  • Selected text on the current page The message you want to reply to. Never read passively — only when you click Generate.
  • Limited surrounding context A short nearby snippet (not full-page scanning) to keep the reply accurate. Same tab, same moment.
  • Page hostname and title Used only as light context (for example, work app vs social app) when you trigger generation.
  • Your saved Smart Reply Pro profiles Fetched from your account and cached locally. Used only to style the reply as you requested.
  • Storage (local, on-device) Stores your login token and cached profiles on your device only. Nothing is shared with third parties.

Data use is action-based: nothing is sent until you click Generate in the popup. You can sign out at any time from the extension popup to clear stored auth/profile data. Smart Reply Pro does not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

The extension requires a Pro subscription. Once you subscribe on the website, the extension unlocks automatically on your next sign-in — no separate purchase needed.
